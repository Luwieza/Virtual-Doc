Our doctors offer the following services: General check-ups, often referred to as primary care physicians (PCPs) or general practitioners (GPs), provide a wide range of healthcare services, including:

  • Routine Physical Exams: Annual check-ups to assess overall health for ages 5 years to adult.
  • Preventive Care: Vaccinations, screenings (e.g., blood pressure, cholesterol, asthma), and lifestyle counseling.
  • Management of Chronic Conditions: Ongoing care for conditions like asthma, diabetes, and hypertension.
  • Diagnosis and Treatment of Acute Illnesses: Handling common illnesses such as colds, flu, minor injuries, and infections.
  • Referrals to Specialists: Coordinating care with specialists for more complex health issues.
  • Health Education and Counseling: Advice on diet, exercise, stress management, and other health concerns.
  • Routine Laboratory Tests: Blood tests, urine tests, and other diagnostics.
  • Medication Management: Prescribing and monitoring medications.
  • Mental Health Support: Initial assessment and treatment of mental health issues like depression and anxiety.

Here are some examples of specialists and the types of treatments we offer:

  • Cardiologists: Treatment of heart conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, and arrhythmias. Procedures like angioplasty, stent placement, and echocardiograms.
  • Dermatologists: Treatment of skin conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer. Procedures like skin biopsies, mole removal, and cosmetic treatments.
  • Endocrinologists: Management of hormonal disorders like diabetes, thyroid diseases, and osteoporosis. Treatment plans involving medication, hormone replacement therapy, and lifestyle modifications.
  • Neurologists: Treatment of neurological disorders such as epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and migraines. Diagnostic tests like EEGs, MRIs, and lumbar punctures.
  • Oncologists: Treatment of cancers using chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and immunotherapy. Coordination of care with surgeons and radiologists for comprehensive cancer treatment.
  • Pulmonologists: Treatment of lung and respiratory conditions like asthma, COPD, and pulmonary fibrosis. Diagnostic tests like spirometry, bronchoscopy, and sleep studies.
  • Psychiatrists: Treatment of mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. Therapies including medication management, psychotherapy, and cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT).
  • Urologists: Treatment of urinary tract and male reproductive system issues like urinary incontinence, kidney stones, and prostate problems. Procedures like cystoscopy, prostate biopsy, and surgical interventions.

In South Africa, online doctors and telemedicine services have become increasingly popular, especially following the COVID-19 pandemic. These services allow patients to consult with healthcare professionals remotely, typically via video calls, phone calls, or chat platforms.

  • Accessibility and Convenience: Online doctor services provide greater accessibility for people living in remote or rural areas where healthcare facilities might be scarce. We also offer convenience for those with busy schedules, reducing the need for travel and waiting times.
  • Range of Services: Telemedicine in South Africa covers a broad range of services including general consultations, follow-up appointments, prescription renewals, mental health support, and specialist consultations.
